Privacy Policy

CodeMed Group ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the CodeMed Group platform and API.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, company name, and password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never store plaintext passwords).
  • API usage data: API key identifiers (hashed), request timestamps, query text (for audit logging), response times, and IP addresses.
  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, company, and message content submitted via our contact form.
  • Session data: Browser session tokens used to maintain your logged-in state.

What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined under HIPAA. The platform is designed for medical billing guidance and coding intelligence — not for storing patient records. Do not submit patient identifiers, names, dates of birth, Social Security numbers, or other PHI through the platform.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your access to the platform.
  • Provide API services, rate limiting, and usage metering.
  • Improve platform performance and answer quality.
  • Respond to support requests and demo inquiries.
  • Comply with legal obligations and CMS program integrity requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Anthropic: Query text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of this data.
  • Infrastructure providers: Railway (hosting), SQLite (database). Data is stored in the United States.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including hashed passwords, HTTPS encryption in transit, and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure.

Cookies and Sessions

We use server-side sessions (Flask sessions) to maintain your authentication state. No third-party tracking cookies are used on the platform.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data by contacting us at privacy@codemedgroup.com.

HIPAA Notice

CodeMed Group is a software-as-a-service tool. A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is available for customers on Growth and Enterprise plans. If you require a BAA, please contact us before submitting any PHI through the platform.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice in the platform.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at privacy@codemedgroup.com or via our contact form.
